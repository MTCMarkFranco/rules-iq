Section 3.2 — Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for a personal loan under this program, applicants must meet all of the following requirements:

1. The applicant must be at least 18 years of age (19 in British Columbia, New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, Newfoundland and Labrador, Northwest Territories, Nunavut, and Yukon).

2. The applicant must be a Canadian citizen, a permanent resident of Canada, or hold a valid work permit with at least 12 months remaining.

3. The applicant's gross annual income must be at least $25,000 CAD. For self-employed applicants, a two-year average of net business income will be used.

4. The applicant's Gross Debt Service (GDS) ratio must not exceed 39%.

5. The applicant's Total Debt Service (TDS) ratio must not exceed 44%.

6. The applicant must have a minimum credit score of 650 (Equifax or TransUnion).

7. The applicant must have been employed in their current position for a minimum of 6 months. Self-employed applicants must demonstrate at least 2 years of continuous business operation.

Note: Exceptions to the above criteria may be considered on a case-by-case basis by the lending committee. Such exceptions are not guaranteed and are at the sole discretion of the committee.
